About me

I am a highly experienced engineer with a proven track record of driving tangible business impact through the use of data. My work spans industries ranging from intelligence collection and public opinon research, to cloud computing and software-as-a-service. Throughout my career, I have leveraged my skillset to tackle complex data challenges and implement scalable, cost-effective solutions.

In my current role, I work with technical, business, and executive stakeholders to solve complex data challenges. My responsibilities require technical know-how, effective communication, and a strong focus on business impact.

I specialize in designing data pipelines and warehousing solutions built with Python/SQL, and deploying them on Google Cloud Platform and Amazon Web Services. I'm comfortable working with a wide range of tools and technologies, including Apache Airflow, Docker, Terraform, Snowflake, and Tableau. I pride myself on producing organized, optimized, and well-documented code that is easy to maintain and extend, and I am always looking for ways to improve.
